Service

I am proud to support my university and the information science field through invited and volunteered service and public outreach. Highlights include:

  • I am heavily involved in ASIS&T: I am on the ARIST editorial board, the European Chapter and 2025 Strategic Planning Committee, and have chaired and juried numerous awards. In 2024 I was awarded Distinguished Membership.
  • I recently advised the EU Fundamental Rights Agency on Assessing high-risk AI and previously advised on a report for the Dutch Government about the responsibilities of online intermediaries in the EU Digital Services Act.
  • I was an associate editor for ACM Journal on Responsible Computing from its inception.
  • I have co-organised numerous conferences, tracks, and workshops.
