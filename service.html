Service

I am proud to support my university and the information science field through invited and volunteered service and public outreach. Recent highlights include:

  • I joined the editorial boards of ARIST and the new ACM Journal on Responsible Computing.
  • I co-organised IST’22, the PIM Workshop at ASIST’22, and the poster track at ISIC’22.
  • For ASIS&T I juried numerous awards, served on the European Chapter, and wrote a Member Spotlight.
  • I advised on a report for the Dutch Government about the European Commission’s Digital Services Act.
  • In my department I serve on the dept council, organise semiannual doctoral colloquiua, and recently hosted Prof. William Jones as a visiting scholar.
