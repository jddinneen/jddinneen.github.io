Prospective doctoral and post-doctoral supervisees: positions at the University of Borås are advertised and applied to here. If contacting me about externally funded positions or visits, please include your academic CV and a short proposal outlining your project’s: problem statement and gaps in existing literature, research questions, foreseeable methods, relevance to my research, and timeline for completion. Unfortunately I cannot respond to requests that do not provide the above or that lack relevance to my research. Unfunded positions are not currently possible.
