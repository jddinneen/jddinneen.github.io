Research

My current research interests are primarily in information ethics (including AI & data ethics) and in personal information management (overlapping info organisation and retrieval). In short, I try to improve people’s everyday lives in the information society (e.g., through systems, services, and perspectives), which necessarily requires integrating technical, social, and philosophical research methods.

A complete list of my publications can be found on Google Scholar.

Current projects

  • AI in information services: libraries & AI, information & data ethics, the information society
  • Data-driven file management tools: personal information management, info organisation and retrieval, human-computer interaction
  • AI Future Lab @ L3S: AI ethics, data ethics, ethical auditing
