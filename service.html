Service

I am proud to support my university and the wider information science field through invited and volunteered service and public outreach. Recent highlights include:

  • I co-organised IST’22, the PIM Workshop at ASIST’22, and the poster track at ISIC’22.
  • I joined the editorial boards of the soon-to-relaunch ARIST, the soon-to-launch ACM Journal on Responsible Computing, and the recently launched Data Science & Informetrics.
  • I juried various ASIST awards and served as Webmaster for ASIST’s European Chapter and SIG-HFIS.
  • I advised on a report for the Government of the Netherlands about the European Commission’s Digital Services Act and presented at a stakeholder workshop.
  • I had the pleasure to speak about email archives at the New Zealand National Library.
