Service

I am proud to support my university and the information science field through invited and volunteered service and public outreach. For example:

  • I have been volunteering for ASIS&T for over a decade, such as the European Chapter, Strategic Planning Committee, SIG-HFIS, and various award juries. In 2024 I was awarded Distinguished Membership.
  • I have advised the EU Fundamental Rights Agency on Assessing high-risk AI and advised on a report for the Dutch Government about the responsibilities of online intermediaries in the EU Digital Services Act.
  • I am a regional editor for Information Research. I have previously been on the editorial boards of ARIST, ACM Journal on Responsible Computing, and Data Science and Informetrics.
  • I have co-organised numerous conferences, tracks, and workshops.
