Research

My current research interests are primarily in information ethics and personal information management. In short, I try to improve people’s everyday lives in the information society (e.g., through systems, services, and perspectives), which necessarily requires integrating technical, social, and philosophical research methods.

A complete list of my publications can be found on Google Scholar.
