Teaching

I enjoy imparting technical skills and reflective, compassionate attitudes to current and future information professionals with the goal of enabling them to be competent and critical leaders of the information society.

Prospective doctoral and post-doctoral supervisees: positions at the University of Borås are advertised and applied to here. If you need to contact me about doc/post-doc supervision please include your academic CV and a short proposal outlining the following about your intended project: problem statement and gaps in the existing literature, research questions, foreseeable methods, relevance to my research (and if applicable to the University of Borås), and a timeline for completion. I cannot respond to requests that do not provide the above or that lack relevance to my research.
