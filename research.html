Research

My current research interests are primarily in information ethics (including AI & data ethics) and in personal information management (overlapping info organisation and retrieval). In short, I seek to find ways to better support people in their everyday lives in the information society (e.g., through improved systems and services), which necessarily requires integrating technical, social, and philosophical research methods.

A complete list of my publications can be found on my Google Scholar page.

Current projects

  • AI & information services: libraries & AI, AI & info ethics, LIS values & identity
  • AI Future Lab @ L3S: AI & data ethics, AI for personalised medicine, ethical auditing
  • Data-driven file management tools: personal information management, info organisation and retrieval, human-computer interaction
  • Hidden Heritage: personal information management, cultural heritage, information behaviour
