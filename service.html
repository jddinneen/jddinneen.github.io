Service

I am proud to support my university and the wider information science field through invited and volunteered service and public outreach. Recent highlights include:

  • I advised on a report for the Dutch Ministry of Economic Affairs and Climate Policy about the European Commission’s Digital Services Act, and presented my perspective on information society policies at a workshop for govt and industry stakeholders.
  • I had the pleasure to speak at the New Zealand National Library.
  • I am a section editor for Open Information Science, an editorial board member for Data Science & Informetrics, and guest edited a special issue of Education for Information. I also co-chaired the poster track at ISIC’22.
  • I am closely involved in ASIS&T, for example in jurying awards and serving as Webmaster for the European Chapter and SIG-HFIS.
  • I regularly provide peer review for international journals (e.g. JASIST, JDOC), conferences (e.g. ACM CHI, ASIST), and national funding programmes (e.g. Canadian NSERC).
