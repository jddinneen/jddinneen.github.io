Service

I am proud to support my university and the wider information science field through invited and volunteered service and public outreach. Recent highlights include:

  • I have been an invited juror for ASIS&T awards and invited guest editor for Education for Information.
  • For the last few years I have been an officer on the ASIS&T special interest group for the History and Foundations of Information Science (SIG HFIS) and a section editor for Open Information Science.
  • I regularly provide peer review for various international journals (e.g. JASIST) and conferences (e.g. ACM CHI).
  • In 2020 I had the pleasure to speak at the New Zealand National Library.
