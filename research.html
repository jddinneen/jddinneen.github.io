Research

My current research interests are primarily in information ethics and personal information management (PIM). In short, I try to create knowledge that can improve people’s everyday lives in the information society (e.g., through systems, services, and perspectives), which necessarily requires integrating technical, social, and philosophical research methods.

A complete list of my publications can be found on Google Scholar and my scholarly code is on GitHub.

Current projects

  • Long-term information management: PIM, sustainability, death
    • Literature review of information management and death
  • Ethical information management: libraries & AI, data privacy, info & society

  • AI Future Lab @ L3S: AI ethics & auditing
