Research

My research interests are primarily in information ethics and personal information management. Currently I am especially focused on the intersection of those topics with post-mortem data management and artificial intelligence. I try to create knowledge to aid responsible computing and information management, which necessarily requires integrating technical, social, and philosophical research methods.

My publications can be found on Google Scholar and my scholarly code is on GitHub.

Current topics & projects

  • Long-term information management:
    • Literature review of information management and death
    • Upcoming edited book on how information supports ageing and legacy
    • Short paper on the importance of deletion
  • AI & ethics in the information society
    • Handbook chapter on challenges of the information society
    • Article on ethical evaluation of AI in libraries
    • Short paper on the ethics of digital housekeeping
